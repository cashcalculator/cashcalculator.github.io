Understanding 401k Employer Matching

Many employers match a percentage of your 401k contributions, effectively giving you free money for retirement. For example, if your employer offers a 50% match up to 6% of your salary, they'll contribute $0.50 for every $1.00 you contribute, up to 6% of your salary.
