Understanding Pension Benefits

A defined benefit pension plan provides a specific monthly benefit at retirement based on your salary history and years of service. The formula typically multiplies your final average salary by your years of service and a benefit multiplier (usually 1.5% - 2.5%).

Replacement Rate: The percentage of your pre-retirement income that your pension will replace. Financial experts often recommend aiming for a 70-80% replacement rate from all sources (pension, Social Security, savings).
